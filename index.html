Terri Burns , WOMEN@FORBES I offer business, leadership and life tips using data visualizations. I take my earphones with me everywhere: to work, to the gym, to grab carton of milk around the corner, sometimes even to bed. I recently moved from New York City to San Francisco for a job as a product manager at a tech company, earphones and all. Just after settling into the hustle and bustle of Silicon Valley, a work trip popped up that required me to head cross-country, back to my old stomping grounds in NYC. When my Forbes editor asked me to chronicle a day in my life, I knew a day from this work trip would be a good one: between the chaos of traveling, speaking at an event and gearing up for a new project at work, I had my tunes with me to mark every step of the way.
